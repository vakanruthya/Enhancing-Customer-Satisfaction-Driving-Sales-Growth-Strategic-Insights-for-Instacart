## Enhancing Customer Satisfaction & Driving Sales Growth
Strategic Insights for Instacart -
This project analyzes customer behavior, product demand patterns, and reorder trends in Instacart's grocery platform to identify strategies that improve customer satisfaction and increase sales.

Using customer analytics, competitor analysis, and machine learning–based product recommendations, the project proposes data-driven strategies to increase customer acquisition, improve reorder rates, and boost average order value (AOV).

The analysis combines business analytics and machine learning techniques to generate actionable insights for online grocery platforms.

## Project Overview

Instacart is one of the leading online grocery delivery platforms, partnering with 1,400+ retailers and covering approximately 85% of the U.S. grocery market. 

Despite strong market presence, Instacart faces increasing competition from platforms such as:

Amazon Fresh

Walmart+

Shipt

The goal of this project is to analyze customer purchasing patterns and platform performance to identify growth opportunities.

## Business Problem

Although Instacart operates in a growing $95B online grocery market, it faces several challenges:

High competition from Amazon and Walmart

Delivery cost perception among users

Inconsistent discount strategies

Regional service quality variability

Traffic share indicates there is room for improvement in user engagement and customer retention.

5. Reorder Behavior Analysis

Key insights:

Produce accounts for 32% of reorders

Dairy and Eggs account for 18%

Beverages and snacks also contribute significantly

Top reordered items:

Bananas

Organic strawberries

Organic avocados

Organic whole milk 

This indicates that fresh essentials drive repeat purchases.

6. Sales Pattern Analysis

Sales patterns reveal:

Peak ordering hours: 11 AM – 7 PM

Highest activity on Monday and Sunday

Most reorders occur at 7-day intervals, indicating weekly shopping habits

Reorder frequency decreases significantly after 30 days, suggesting strong weekly purchasing behavior. 

## Machine Learning Implementation

To increase average order value (AOV), the project implemented association rule mining algorithms to identify frequently purchased product combinations.

Algorithms tested:

Apriori

H-Mine

FP-Growth

Best Model: FP-Growth

FP-Growth performed best because it:

efficiently handles large datasets

identifies strong product associations

enables real-time product recommendations

These recommendations help drive:

cross-selling

bundle promotions

personalized suggestions 

Business Strategy Recommendations -
Increase Customer Acquisition

Strategies include:

Region-specific pricing strategies

Increase Reorders

## Key recommendations

1. Product Bundling

Bundle commonly purchased items:

Example:

Bananas + Organic Milk + Strawberries
2. Subscription-Based Reorders

Offer automated reorder reminders based on:

weekly purchase cycles

monthly replenishment patterns

3. Cross-Sell Recommendations

Promote complementary products during checkout.

4. Upsell Low-Demand Categories

Encourage purchases of underperforming departments such as frozen foods and bakery items.

## Key Insights

Fresh produce dominates customer demand

Weekly shopping patterns drive reorders

Personalized recommendations increase basket size

Bundling strategies can significantly increase AOV

Delivery cost perception impacts customer acquisition
